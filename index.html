Feature

Appointment Booking & Scheduling

Patients can book appointments and view scheduled visits from App. A provider can set up his calendar schedule.

Payment Collection

Provider can collect payment from the patient. The App can be integrated with any payment channel.

Video Consultation

App provides live video streaming supported with multiple devices. During the video, a provider can see the patient profile to provide better care.

Patient Charting & SOAP Notes

Provider can take notes while having a video call with patients. App provides quick access to drugs and labs to generate prescription in no time.

Practice Management

App allows managing end to end operations of a practice. App provides multiple workflows to optimize practice management.

Electronic Prescription

Provider can generate electronic prescription with the App, and the patient will be able to download it using a patient mobile App.
