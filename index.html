Patient Flow

1
Search Provider

Patient can connect directly with provider based on their speciality and shared code.

2
Select Time Slot

Patients can view providers' calendar and book appointments to get started with care.

3
Authenticate

Patient can authenticate using mobile number or email address and create account.

4
Pay Consultation Fee

Patient can either provide insurance information or make full payment of appointment fee before proceed.

5
Upload Records

Patients can upload previous medical records, lab reports , radiology and prescription for better understand of patient medical history.

6
Enter Virtual Waiting Room

Patients can test the microphone and video camera in the waiting room before actually joining the appointment.

7
Join Video Consultation

App provider high quality real time video calling for patient and provider to discuss medical issues.

8
Download Prescription

Providers sign off SOAP notes after appointment, patients can download prescription and take medicines.
