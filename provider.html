Create account

Get started with teledocs in mins by quickly creating account with teledocs. App sends account activation link to email. Once you confirm you are ready to take patient online.

Setup Availability

Setup your availability to take appointments. You add breaks, block days and even change availability base on requirements. Also allow you to add buffer between appointments. Your staff can also manage your availability.

Create Patient

Create patients or even you can import patients into teledocs to get started quickly. Patient gets activation link once account created, patient can download patient mobile apps to get started with platform.

Book Appointment

You can book appointment by entering patient name, date time and reason for appointment. Both patient and provider get notification on booking and also app has inbuilt reminder notification for appointments.

Take Video Consultation

Teledocs provider virtual waiting room for both patients and providers to test audio and video before call, also messaging functionality to handle anyconnect issues. Teledocs provides high quality video streaming with ability to add more guest to call.

Prescriptions and Notes

During video consultation you can take notes and also prescribe patients without going back and forth. App has master database of drugs and labs to faster prescribe patient in no time.

Generate Bills and Get Paid

Provider can setup consultation charges per appointment types and generate bill with single click after appointments. Also provider can setup his bank or any payment wallet to accept bills.
