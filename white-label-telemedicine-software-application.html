Frequently Asked Questions on White Label Program

What is white label partner program?

Under white label program, you can own a full feature telemedicine platform with your custom branding and you will have rights to sell to your customers and generate revenue.

How can I join as a partner?

We just need you to fill out the online White Label Partner Program Registration Form, our team will contact you and get you started with white label program.

No you don’t need to have a technology team, we will provide you all technical support as part of white label program.
