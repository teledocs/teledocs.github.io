    As a Provider As a Patient

Create Account

Patient can create account with mobile number or email. App sends activation OPT or link to get started with platform. Teledocs provider patient easy to use mobile application.

Connect to Provider

Teledocs assign unique code to each provider. Patient can connect to provider only if they have code associated with provider. Platform respect security of each provider and patient

Book Appointment

Patient can book appointment with any connect provider or provider group. Patient gets consistent follow up notification and information about appointments and updates if any.

Make Payment

Patient can make payment for appointment with bank or any payment wallet. App providers payment bills with details.

Video Consultation

Patient can take video consultation or audio call base on choice with cool patient mobile app. Patient can enjoy high quality video streaming with comfort of home.

Prescriptions & Notes

Once appointment is done patient receives prescriptions and notes created during appointments. Patient has options to download and get it from labs.

Fully compliant with indian Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020. We provide end-end solution for provider to setup virtual clinic and deliver care without any issue. We are offering platform for free to fight with coronavirus
