Create Account

Patients can create an account with a mobile number or email. App sends activation OPT or links to get started with a platform. Teledocs provider-patient easy to use a mobile application.

Connect to Provider

Teledocs assign unique codes to each provider. Patients can connect to the provider only if they have a code associated with a provider. Platform respects the security of each provider and patient.

Book Appointment

Patients can book appointment with any connect provider or provider group. A patient gets consistent follow-up notifications and information about appointments and updates, if any.

Make Payment

Patient can make payment for appointment with bank or any payment wallet. App providers payment bills with details.

Video Consultation

Patients can take video consultation or audio call base on choice with a cool patient mobile app. A patient can enjoy high-quality video streaming with the comfort of home.

Prescriptions & Notes

Once the appointment is done, A patient receives prescriptions and notes created during appointments. Patient has options to download and get it from labs.
